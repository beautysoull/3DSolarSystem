Instructions for computers:

  • Left mouse button: Move the camera up/down, left/right
  • Right mouse button: Rotate the camera around the center
  • Mouse wheel: Zoom in/out

Instructions for mobile devices:

  • One finger: Swipe up/down, left/right to rotate the camera
  • Two fingers: Pinch to zoom in/out
  • Two finger swipe: Move the camera around the screen
